How does the online Puja service work? (การฝากถวายออนไลน์ทำงานอย่างไร?) +
Simply choose your desired deity and offering package from our store. During checkout, provide your name, birthdate, and personal prayer. Our priests in Thailand will perform the authentic ceremony on your behalf.
When will I receive my E-Certificate? (ฉันจะได้รับ E-Certificate เมื่อไหร่?) +
After the ceremony is completed (usually within 3-5 business days of your order), we will send high-quality photos of the ceremony and your personalized E-Certificate directly to your email address.

Yes, absolutely. All ceremonies are strictly performed according to ancient Vedic and Hindu traditions by ordained priests at our verified locations in Thailand.

Yes! You can enter your loved one's name and birthdate during the checkout process. This makes a beautiful and meaningful spiritual gift.
